# SCRAPER_POLICY.md

## Rules
- Respect robots.txt and terms of use.
- Use conservative rate limits and retries.
- Avoid login-protected/private areas without authorization.
- Avoid collecting sensitive personal data.
- Collect only public business-relevant information.
- Log domains and run summaries in `workspace\ops`.

## Data Scope
- Company pages, public job pages, public contact role pages.
- No credential stuffing, no anti-bot bypass techniques.

## Safety
- Stop scraping if legal/ethical ambiguity appears.
- Ask user before broadening scope.

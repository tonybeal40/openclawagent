Please note

We are aware that there are fraudulent hiring schemes aimed at candidates to collect financial and sensitive personal data under false pretenses. Siemens will never ask for financial information during the selection process. Siemens interviews will always come from an official Siemens email address and will never come from Gmail, etc. Please be cautious. If you feel you are a victim of fraudulent activity, please report it to the proper authorities.

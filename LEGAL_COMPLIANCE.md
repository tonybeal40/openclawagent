# LEGAL_COMPLIANCE.md

## Purpose
This environment is for research, productivity, and internal business operations.

## Acceptable Data Sources
- Public company websites and public job postings
- Publicly available business information
- User-provided documents/data with permission

## Prohibited / Restricted
- Login-protected scraping without authorization
- Circumventing paywalls or access controls
- Collecting sensitive personal data without lawful basis/consent

## robots.txt and Site Terms
- Respect robots.txt and website terms of service.
- Use conservative request rates and avoid abusive traffic patterns.

## Copyright Compliance
- Do not reproduce protected content in bulk.
- Summarize and transform content for internal use.

## Privacy Considerations
- GDPR/US privacy alignment: data minimization, purpose limitation, retention controls.
- Remove or redact personal data when not essential.

## Logging and Audit
- Keep action logs and restore reports in `workspace\ops` / `workspace\logs`.
- Redact secrets and personal identifiers where possible.

## Disclaimer
This system is intended for internal research and operations support, not legal advice.

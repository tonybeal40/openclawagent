    What Our Emerson Employees Say

    We encourage employees to think big, ask the hard questions, and challenge conventional thinking—without the fear of failure.
    Let’s meet some of those thinkers, doers, and connectors.

    Marija Stipetic-Lovaas, Senior Strategic Marketing Manager, Norway

    “While successful culture can look and feel like magic, culture is built on a set of relationships and practices. It is something we do. I work in a culture that allows me to have a voice and hears that voice.”

    Robert Axtell, Maintenance Manager, USA

    "I joined Emerson, because of the high reputation of the Appleton Foundry and its attention to innovation and cleanliness. The environment that has been created here is unlike any other professional experience I’ve had and the camaraderie between our group is great.”

    Sri Kumar, Vice President, Projects & Services, Singapore

    “One of my proudest moments in my Emerson career was hiring fresh university graduates in Saudi Arabia as part of the Saudization strategy. This strategic move not only demonstrated Emerson's commitment to our local customers but also paved the way for winning significant projects and business in Saudi Arabia.”

    Rikiyah Fletcher, Sales Representative, USA

    “Emerson and my direct management have supported me numerously in my efforts to advance my career. I've had the opportunity to not only participate as an ERG leader within Black Employee Alliance, but also to travel to attend trainings, summits, conferences, and most recently supporting my efforts to attend graduate school for my MBA.”

    Cheri Wong, Senior Director – Quality & EHS, Singapore

    “Surrounded by supportive leaders and co-workers, I have had many opportunities to collaborate with amazing colleagues to deliver positive outcomes in a psychologically safe environment where I can be my authentic self.  That is what keeps me excited and motivated at work every day.”

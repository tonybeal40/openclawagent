    Pro Tips
    

      

    • Keep important text in the center 80% — edges get cropped on mobile
      • 

    • Use high contrast text — LinkedIn's feed has a white background
      • 

    • Profile banners are partially covered by your photo on the bottom-left
      • 

    • Article covers show a gradient overlay at the bottom where the title appears
      • 

    • Export as PNG for text-heavy images, JPG for photos
      • 

    • LinkedIn sweet spot for posts: 800–1400 characters
      •

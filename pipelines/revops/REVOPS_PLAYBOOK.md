# REVOPS PLAYBOOK (v1)

## Mission Lens
Prioritize companies where RevOps/system improvements can unlock measurable revenue lift within 1-2 quarters.

## High-Value Signals
- Active hiring for SDR/AE/RevOps/Sales Ops
- Rapid growth indicators (new funding, expansion announcements)
- Multi-product or multi-segment GTM motion
- Evidence of CRM/process complexity
- Long or unclear buying journey

## Low-Value Signals
- Solo founder or very small (<5) teams
- Local-only service business with no clear B2B sales process
- Agency models with low RevOps leverage

## Scoring Guide (0-100)
- Hiring & growth signals: 0-30
- Sales complexity: 0-20
- Pain indicators: 0-30
- ICP fit: 0-20

## Pain Indicators to Flag
- Inconsistent pipeline visibility
- Poor CRM adoption hygiene
- Weak lead qualification process
- Forecast volatility
- Sales cycle drag

## Messaging Angles
1. Pipeline visibility and stage integrity
2. CRM implementation/adoption
3. Lead qualification and routing
4. Sales-cycle acceleration
5. Forecast quality and operating cadence

## Daily Success Definition
- >= 5 qualified companies
- >= 15 decision makers
- >= 5 outreach drafts
- All outputs pass schema and include evidence block
